About Poornima College of Engineering
Poornima College of Engineering is the main campus of Poornima Group of Colleges, established by the Shanti Education Society in year 2000 with a student strength of 180.It is a member of the IBM Software Centre of Excellence. Rajasthan.

Poornima Group of Colleges comprises Poornima College of Engineering (established 2000), Poornima Institute of Engineering & Technology (established 2007), and Poornima Group of Institutions (established 2009).
The College runs a BTech programme in six specialisations of Engineering; Civil Engineering (CE), Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE), Electrical Engineering (EE), Mechanical Engineering (ME) and Information Technology (IT). Apart from this, it also offers Value Added Programs in collaboration with IBM.

These programmes are offered under various emerging technologies such as Cloud, Mobility, Analytics and Cybersecurity.

Poornima College of Engineering admission process is conducted online through the official website of PGC. Admission to BTech programme is entrance-based. Details for the same are as follows:

1) BTech admissions are based on REAP/ JEE Main scores.
2) Candidates, who have completed Class 12 in Science stream from a recognised board, can apply for BTech programme

frequently ask questions
Q. How can I apply for Poornima College of Engineering admissions?
A. The admission process is conducted online through the official website of PGC. Candidates need to register for REAP counselling, followed by the College registration
Q. What all programmes are offered by Poornima College of Engineering?
A. At present, the College offers a BTech programme in six different specialisations.
Q. What is the eligibility criteria for BTech programme at Poornima College of Engineering?
A. Candidates, who have completed Class 12 in Science stream from a recognised board, can apply for BTech programme
Q. What is the seat intake for BTech programme at Poornima College of Engineering?
A. The total seat intake for the programme is 660 students.
Q. What all specialisations are offered under BTech programme at Poornima College of Engineering?
A. BTech is offered in six different specialisations, namely Civil Engineering (CE), Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE), Electrical Engineering (EE), Mechanical Engineering (ME) and Information Technology (IT).
Q. What all entrance exams are accepted by Poornima College of Engineering?
A. The College accepts JEE Main/ REAP exams for BTech admissions
Q. What is the total fee for BTech programme at Poornima College of Engineering?
A. The total fee for BTech programme is Rs 3.15 lakh
Q. Is there any relaxation available for reserved category candidates at Poornima College of Engineering admission process?
A. Yes, reserved category candidates will get a relaxation of 5 per cent in the minimum qualifying marks
Q. What is the seat intake for BTech (ME) at Poornima College of Engineering?
A. BTech (ME) has a seat intake of 120 students
Q. What all subjects were taught in BTech (CE) at Poornima College of Engineering?
A. BTech (CE) will cover the in-depth study of subjects like Physics, Mathematics, Geology and Hydrology
Q. Which university is Poornima College of Engineering affiliated to?
A. The College is affiliated to the Rajasthan Technical University
Q. Does Poornima College of Engineering offers Value Added Programs?
A. Yes, it offers Value Added Programs in collaboration with IBM
